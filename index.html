Our Story

Sanyang Shisha Factory was established six years ago. We devote ourselves to make high quality shisha accessories. We have a store based in Yiwu, Zhejiang, China. Most of the business we have done were offline. To provide better service and fast respond we’ve decided to extend our business to online. That’s why we established SANYANG NETWORK CO., LTD. We are working on this online part really seriously and wish to get more satisfaction from our customers. Furthermore, our brand new online store will be released soon.
